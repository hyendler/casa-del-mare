About Casa Del Mare

Hear the waves in this Aptos vacation home, located across the street from the neighborhood beach entrance to the Seacliff and Pot Belly beaches. Walk down the picturesque Beachgate path, or drive 0.2mi and use provided California State Park pass for free access & parking.

Brand new remodel & furniture!!! New kitchen, baths and all new furniture and mattresses.

2 bedrooms, 1.5 baths - sleeps 6, with California king bed, queen bed, and pull out queen sofa bed in the living-room. New kitchen with granite counters, remodeled bathrooms and an outdoor warm shower.

The kitchen is fully equipped and the house includes washer and dryer, an outdoor hot water shower, cable TV, WiFi, DVD player, stereo with CD player, and lots of games.

Just bring your bathing suit. Everything else is provided: linens, beach towels, chairs, umbrella and toys.

Also included are:

  • CA state park vehicle pass to visit over 20 local state parks.
  • 2 member guest passes to the Monterey Bay Aquarium. Save time by skipping a long line and using the member entrance!

Seacliff beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in the area. It is wide, 19+ mile long, and in low tide you can walk 1.5 mi to Capitola. Spend your days swimming, boogie boarding, and surfing with the dolphins, whales, seals and shorebirds to keep you company.

This is a great location for visiting Monterrey, Carmel, Capitola, Santa Cruz, 17 Mile Drive or just relaxing on the beach.
